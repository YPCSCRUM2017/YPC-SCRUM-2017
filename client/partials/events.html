Nov. 3rd

YPC Kick-Off @ The Loft

Please join the Young Professional Council in the heart of downtown San Jose for their inaugural kick-off happy hour! Enjoy the opportunity to mix and mingle with other like-minded young philanthropists while learning more about why The YPC is dedicated towards supporting the future, state of the art Women and Children's Center at Santa Clara County's largest and only public hospital, Santa Clara Valley Medical Center.



Meet the Executive Committee of the YPC to hear about how we support the mission of the Valley Medical Center, the upcoming events YPC will be hosting in 2017 and how you can get involved! You will also have the opportunity to chat with Valley Medical Center professionals including the CEO of The VMC Foundation, Chris Wilder to hear all about the exciting future of the SCVMC!

Each ticket will include a drink, appetizers and entry to win raffle prizes including:

  • San Jose Sharks tickets
  • Award Winning Bottles of Wine
  • Gift Cards
  • And more!

*Attire will be Business Casual
